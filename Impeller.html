Background

Two Request for Proposals (RFPs) have been received from an aerospace manufacturing company. A number of centrifugal pumps are required, and your design team wishes to gain the contract to supply the pumps. There are not sufficient resources for the company to redesign and manufacture new versions of both the casing and rotor, so the decision has been taken to use the existing design of casing and to concentrate on improving rotor performance. It would be too costly to perform development work on pumps of realistic sizes, so the requirement is to design a rotor for a scaled model. Its performance can be measured on a test rig and, using dimensional analysis, a confident prediction can then be made on how the real-size version would perform.

Request for Proposal

Pratt & Whitney Canada (PWC) is a world-leading aerospace manufacturer, with a history of producing aircraft engines since the 1950s. Our success is largely based on continuous innovations in product design and manufacturing methods.
